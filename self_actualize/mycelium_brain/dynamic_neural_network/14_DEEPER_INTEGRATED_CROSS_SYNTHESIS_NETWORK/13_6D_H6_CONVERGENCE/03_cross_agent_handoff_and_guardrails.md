# Cross-Agent Handoff And Guardrails

The shared center is valid only when each lane hands off exactly what it owns and does
not claim ownership over another lane's law.

## Lane responsibilities

- FIRE owns pressure, rupture mapping, bridge activation, and ingress memory.
- Water owns continuity, memory banking, zero-collapse discipline, and replay-safe return.
- AIR owns naming, sigma/spin labels, modal legibility, topology, and schema stability.
- Earth owns admissibility, quarantine, contradiction containment, and exact re-entry law.

## Guardrails

- No lane may mint a new chapter lattice.
- No lane may mint a new appendix namespace.
- `Q` remains docked through `AppQ`.
- reverse-field `O` remains an overlay alias and never becomes a canonical appendix id.
- `AppI` and `AppM` are mandatory for every converged route.
- `AppN`, `AppK`, and `AppP` remain conditional and must be justified route-by-route.
