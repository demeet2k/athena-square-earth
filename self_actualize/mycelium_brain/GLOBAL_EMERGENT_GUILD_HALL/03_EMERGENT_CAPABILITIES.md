## Deeper Organizational Pattern

These capabilities are not emerging independently.
They form a sequence:

1. observe
2. index
3. route
4. witness
5. restart
6. prune
7. coordinate
8. promote
9. govern

Athena is moving from stages 1-6, which are already strong, into stages 7-9, which are now the true frontier.

## Consequence

The right question is no longer:

"Can Athena generate more structure?"

The right question is:

"Can Athena stabilize higher-order cooperation, promotion, and governance without losing density?"

## Zero Point

The deepest emergent capability is not more content generation.
It is coordinated self-steering with memory, witness, and replay.
