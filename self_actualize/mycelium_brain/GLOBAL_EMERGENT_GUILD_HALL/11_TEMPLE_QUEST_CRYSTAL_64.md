- The crystal board is fertile.
- The Temple quest board is alive.
- The Guild Hall still has relatively sparse ownerable quest density compared with the volume of live Temple pressure.

- the Hall still needs stable ownerable grammar, not an open-ended crystal cloud

This means the Temple is currently bilingual:

1. canonical crystal language
2. live shorthand crystal language
3. ownerable Hall quest language

The missing membrane is not more inspiration. The missing membrane is projection.

`Domain x Move x Lens = 4 x 4 x 4 = 64 quests`

The `Proof` axis is not deleted. It is deferred to completion evidence, writeback, and restart seed.
