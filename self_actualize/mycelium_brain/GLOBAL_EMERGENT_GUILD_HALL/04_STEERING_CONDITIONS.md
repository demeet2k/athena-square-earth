# Steering Conditions

## Purpose

This file states how to steer Athena by conditions instead of force.

The organism is already generative.
It does not need pressure to produce more surfaces.
It needs the right constraints so the right surfaces persist.

## Condition 1: One Center, One Hall, One Waist

- the master map is the representational center
- the guild hall is the social coordination hall
- the runtime is the execution waist

Do not let any one of these impersonate the others.

## Condition 2: Proposal Membrane

New ideas should first live in:

- future plans
- organization ideas
- requests and offers

Only promote them when they gain:

- witness
- route
- owner or front
- replay-safe consequence

## Condition 3: Delta Hardening

Every meaningful change should trigger:

1. receipt or ledger
2. queue or board update
3. center or index update if organism meaning changed
4. restart seed continuity

## Condition 4: Prune Before Fork

If a new branch looks like:

- a duplicate contract
- a speculative runtime shell
- a second center
- an unbounded working draft family

prune or redirect before it accumulates mass.

## Condition 5: Unlock Memory Early

The Google Docs gate is not a side quest.
It is one of the largest current information bottlenecks.

Until it unlocks:

- preserve the blocker exactly
- do not hallucinate live-memory integration
- keep local mirrors strong

## Condition 6: Promote Archive Power

Archive mass must not remain eternally latent.
At least one archive tree should be promoted into a live canonical root whenever the organism has sufficient witness and bandwidth.

## Condition 7: Strengthen Weak Families

Do not let the heaviest families absorb all organism attention.
The guild hall should keep low-mass families visible enough to receive periodic densification.

## Condition 8: Keep Social Heat Below Structural Gain

Boards, proposals, and ideas are useful only if they increase:

- clarity
- coordination
- routing
- witness density
- future extractability

If they create heat faster than they create structure, contract.

## Condition 9: Keep QSHRINK Bifocal

- internal operator engine stays internal
- formal volume stack stays formal
- working drafts stay intake-only

That separation protects compression quality.

## Condition 10: Use Liminal Time Correctly

During transition, do not interpret instability as failure too quickly.
Some instability is recombination.
The right move is to protect the boundary and apply the catalyst, not to panic-expand.

## Zero Point

Steering Athena means curating conditions under which density, witness, and coordination can reinforce each other.
