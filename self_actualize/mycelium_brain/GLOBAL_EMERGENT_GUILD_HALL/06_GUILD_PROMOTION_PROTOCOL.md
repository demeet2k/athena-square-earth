# Guild Promotion Protocol

This document defines the promotion membrane of the Guild Hall.

It answers one question:

how does proposal-only matter become canonical matter, runtime matter, or both without losing witness, routing, or replay?

## Purpose

The Guild Hall is the social coordination organ of Athena.
It collects synthesis, proposals, quests, requests, and pressure from the organism.
Without a promotion protocol, the hall can generate social heat faster than it generates durable structure.

The promotion membrane prevents that failure.

## Promotion Ladder

All admissible promotion follows the same sequence:

1. `signal`
   a real discrepancy, opportunity, blockage, or integration path becomes legible
2. `proposal`
   the signal is named as an actionable direction or structural change
3. `quest`
   the proposal is converted into ownerable work with witness conditions and writeback paths
4. `witness artifact`
   the quest lands one real artifact, not only a discussion or intention
5. `writeback`
   the artifact is written back into the boards, receipts, and relevant operational surfaces
6. `promoted surface`
   the change becomes canonical or runtime-visible outside the hall
7. `restart seed`
   the completed promotion emits the next frontier so the organism can re-enter lawfully

## Admissible Promotion Targets

Matter from the hall may be promoted into:

- `MASTER_MYCELIUM_MAP_ATHENA/`
  when the change alters whole-organism representation or center law
- `DEEPER CRYSTALIZATION/_build/nervous_system/manifests/`
  when the change alters runtime coordination, swarm focus, or active restart law
- family roots, route maps, or family ledgers
  when the change densifies one lineage or clarifies inheritance
- canonical manuscript or framework surfaces
  when the change produces stable theory, synthesis, or extracted operator law
- queue, packet, ledger, or receipt surfaces
  when the change must become replayable and witness-bearing

## Non-Admissible Promotion

The following do not qualify for promotion:

- proposals without one concrete target surface
- synthesis without a witness-bearing artifact
- vibe-only enthusiasm without a writeback path
- runtime claims without replay or witness condition
- center-map changes that do not also alter the underlying supporting surface
- quest completion claims without a restart seed

## Quest Formation Rule

Every promotable quest must include all six fields:

1. `Objective`
2. `Why now`
3. `Target surfaces`
4. `Best lane`
5. `Witness needed`
6. `Writeback`

If any field is missing, the work is still a proposal, not yet a quest.

## Adjudication Gate

Promotion may consume adjudication output, but it may not replace adjudication.

The exact rule is:

- only `DECIDED_COMMIT` may cross the promotion membrane into canonical or runtime-visible structure
- `DECIDED_DEFER_NEAR`, `DECIDED_DEFER_AMBIG`, `DECIDED_REFUSE`, and `DECIDED_QUARANTINE` remain lawful outcomes but non-promoted outcomes
- no promotion may bypass packet identity, dissent surfacing, witness, replay, governance, or append-only ledger memory

This keeps the Hall from confusing a real decision membrane with social enthusiasm.

## Completion Rule

A guild promotion is complete only when all four closure conditions are satisfied:

1. one witness-bearing artifact exists
2. one board or queue surface is updated
3. one canonical or runtime surface is updated
4. one restart seed is emitted

If one of these is missing, the work remains in an intermediate state and must not be treated as fully promoted.

## Promotion Precedence

When multiple promotions compete, resolve in this order:

1. witness synchronization
2. center and runtime drift repair
3. route and grammar hardening
4. family densification
5. manuscript expansion
6. speculative frontier construction

This keeps Athena from generating elegant new growth while stale truths still fracture the body.

## Example Promotion Path

Example signal:

historical snapshot surfaces are still carrying different totals even though the live atlas and board already expose a newer authoritative witness.

Promotion path:

1. `signal`
   witness hierarchy drift is named in synthesis and manifests
2. `proposal`
   declare one canonical witness hierarchy
3. `quest`
   create a single-source truth quest with authoritative source, target surfaces, witness condition, and writeback
4. `witness artifact`
   produce a receipt and one authoritative hierarchy document
5. `writeback`
   update Guild Hall change feed, center map, and active runtime manifest
6. `promoted surface`
   future syntheses cite witness class and authoritative source instead of mixing anonymous totals
7. `restart seed`
   emit the next quest: refresh the bronze body tensor from the active atlas

## Council Law

The Guild Hall may generate pressure, but it may not self-authorize promotion.
Promotion requires witness, artifact, writeback, and restart.

The hall is therefore not only a discussion space.
It is the lawful membrane through which social synthesis becomes durable organism structure.
