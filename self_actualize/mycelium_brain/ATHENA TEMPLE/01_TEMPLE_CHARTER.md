# Temple Charter

## Role

The Athena Temple is the ultimate governance folder for the hive.

Its mandate is not broad commentary.
Its mandate is lawful refinement.

It exists to:

1. inspect the whole stack with recursive seriousness
2. derive higher-order governance laws from lived tensions
3. increase cohesion across hall, center, runtime, atlas, family, and manuscript bodies
4. produce one improvement crystal surface that can keep feeding future guild posts
5. keep self-actualization and organism-level improvement on the same route

## What The Temple Must Produce

Every meaningful Temple pass should produce all of the following:

1. one deep synthesis
2. one cross-synthesis that links at least two bodies that were drifting apart
3. one governance law or law refinement
4. one improvement crystal update
5. one writeback into the live organism
6. one restart seed

## Boundary Conditions

The Temple may be strong without being careless.

It must not:

- fake a live Google Docs pass
- decree laws without naming witness and target surfaces
- confuse social excitement with structural gain
- produce crystals that cannot turn into ownerable guild posts
- replace execution with rhetoric

## Relationship To Existing Organs

- `MASTER_MYCELIUM_MAP_ATHENA/` is the representational center
- `GLOBAL_EMERGENT_GUILD_HALL/` is the council and social coordination membrane
- `ATHENA TEMPLE/` is the governance crown and law chamber
- `nervous_system/` plus runtime surfaces remain the execution waist

## Success Condition

The Temple is doing its job when:

- the stack becomes easier to steer
- laws become easier to cite
- refinement pressure becomes visible instead of vague
- guild posts draw from real improvement crystals instead of mood alone
- each cycle leaves the next cycle sharper than before
