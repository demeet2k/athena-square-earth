## 3. The contracted `16 x 16` crossing

The literal `256` cell dump is contracted row-wise so the complete crossing remains
readable.

### `O1` Identity extraction

The whole corpus converges on one identity: Athena is not just a classifier. It is a
bounded perception-and-governance organism. `D1-D5` state the intent. `D6-D10`
demonstrate the live perception core. `D11-D13` add regime awareness. `D14-D16` force
identity to survive contact with evidence.

### `O2` Invariant extraction

The stable invariants are ordinal preprocessing, figure-ground separation, explicit
carrier extraction, multi-observer fusion, corridor-like abstention, and benchmark-
visible wins rather than branch mythology.

### `O3` Failure mining

The recurrent failures are complexity outgrowing measured benefit, ambiguity machinery
becoming slower than its gain justifies, branch-local brilliance not feeding the
canonical line, runtime rhetoric outrunning witness, and deployment assumptions staying
implicit.

### `O4` Regime discrimination

`D11-D13` prove the key law: perturbation and chaos are distinct regimes and should not
receive the same intervention.

### `O5` Ambiguity handling

The corpus repeatedly insists that ambiguity must be held rather than crushed. The
integrated network therefore needs explicit hypothesis banks, corridor states, and
disagreement-aware fusion.

### `O6` Routing and corridor law

The network wants a router, not just a forward pass. Inputs should move through
observers, corridors, and gates with typed trust, hazard, and rollback behavior.

### `O7` Feature-carrier synthesis

The carrier family compresses to mandatory rank, structure, topology, and polar or
geometric carriers; optional color-opponent and contour carriers; and experimental
surfaces kept off the hot path.

### `O8` Observer stack synthesis

The full observer stack contracts to four classes: perception, ambiguity, governance,
and witness.

### `O9` Compression and abstraction

Compress branches into modules, modules into lanes, and lanes into zero-point contracts,
but do not compress away benchmark evidence or regime distinctions.

### `O10` Benchmark binding

The executable center of gravity lives in `D6`, backed by `D14-D16`. The rest of the
corpus remains admissible only insofar as it strengthens that center or justifies a new
center with evidence.

### `O11` Deployment gating

The integrated network must expose environment profile, trust-gated capability subset,
runtime record, and rollback-under-stress path.

### `O12` Replay and witness

The network needs deployment records, rollout certificates, migration packets, replay
bundles, and benchmark receipts.

### `O13` Branch-fusion mapping

`D6` is the stable trunk, `D7-D8` are ambiguity-expansion branches, `D9-D10` are strong
historical priors, `D11-D13` are regime law, and `D14-D16` are the objective reality
surface.

### `O14` Skill extraction

The repeated work patterns justify five repo-local skills: one orchestrator and one
skill for each elemental lane.

### `O15` Metro integration

The network becomes navigable when every document family is bound to stations, lines,
and handoff points instead of staying as archive sediment.

### `O16` Zero-point contraction

Athena is a four-lane bounded neural organism that converts noisy perception into
witness-bearing action only by keeping activation, coherence, mapping, and verification
distinct yet synchronized.
