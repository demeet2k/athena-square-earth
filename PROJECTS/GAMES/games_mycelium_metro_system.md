## Four-Axis Framework

This corpus resolves cleanly into four recurrent axes:

1. `Motion`
   - the actual exercise substrate
   - push-up, v-up, arch-up, squat, lunge, mountain climber, burpee, plank, plyometric, full-body variants
2. `Mechanics`
   - the rule engine that decides what movement gets done
   - chain matching, power cards, coin flips, V-level scaling, health tracking
3. `Myth`
   - the motivational and symbolic wrapper that changes how the same effort is experienced
   - "From Shuffle to Muscle", `Vitalia`, guardians, heroes, event and super cards, warm-up and cool-down encouragement
4. `Membrane`
   - the delivery layer that moves the system between design, print, QR, video, archive, and sale
   - PDFs, zips, QR sheets, landing pages, UPCs, box copy
