The 64 hexagrams are not a SEQUENCE — they are a STATE SPACE.
Imagine a 64-room mansion. Time doesn't walk through the rooms in order 1-2-3-4... Time can move from any room to any other room following specific rules (the changing lines).

Each hexagram contains six lines. Any line can be "changing" (old yang → young yin, or old yin → young yang). This determines which hexagram you move TO from the current state.
0 changing lines: Stay in current hexagram
1 changing line: Move to hexagram that differs by 1 line
2 changing lines: Move to hexagram that differs by 2 lines
etc.
The Movement Probability:
Not all transitions are equally likely. The I Ching encodes a weighted graph where some transitions are more probable than others based on the natural flow of change.
