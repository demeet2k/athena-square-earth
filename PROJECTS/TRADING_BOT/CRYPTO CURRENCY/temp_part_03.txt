THE MAYAN CALENDAR ROUND (52 Years)
LCM(260 Tzolkin, 365 Haab) = 18,980 days = 52 solar years
When both the sacred (260-day) and civil (365-day) calendars return to their starting position simultaneously — a complete synchronization of spiritual and material time.
Every 52 years, the Aztecs performed the New Fire ceremony, extinguishing all flames throughout the empire and relighting them from a single source on a mountaintop — symbolizing the death and rebirth of time itself.
If the ceremony succeeded (the fire was lit), time would continue. If it failed, the universe would end. The entire population would fast, destroy household items, and wait in darkness to see if creation would be renewed.
52 years ≈ one generation. The Calendar Round tracks the complete energy cycle of a human generation.

THE JUPITER CYCLE (12 Years)
Jupiter takes approximately 12 years to orbit the Sun, spending about 1 year in each zodiac sign.
This is why the Chinese zodiac animals cycle every 12 years — they're tracking Jupiter, the "Year Star" (Sui Xing 歲星) in Chinese astronomy.
Jupiter Returns at ages 12, 24, 36, 48, 60... mark major life transitions:
Age 12: End of childhood, puberty, entering the social world
Age 24: Early adulthood, career establishment, first maturity
Age 36: Mid-life assessment, consolidation or crisis
Age 48: Mature power, preparation for elder status
Age 60: Completion of the Chinese cycle, wisdom-holder status

THE SATURN CYCLE (29.5 Years)
Saturn takes approximately 29.5 years to orbit the Sun.
Saturn Returns at ages ~29, ~58, ~87 are traditionally times of reckoning:
Age 29: The end of youth. What structures have you built? Are they authentic? Saturn demands reality checks.
Age 58: The end of productive adulthood. What legacy are you leaving? Time to face mortality.
Age 87: The final accounting. What has been learned? What wisdom can be transmitted?
The first Saturn Return (~29) is often a crisis time — Saturn (the taskmaster, reality-principle) tests whether the identity you've constructed is actually yours or just inherited assumptions. Careers change, marriages end or are tested, life purpose is questioned.

THE VEDIC NAKSHATRAS (27 Lunar Mansions)
360° ÷ 27 = 13°20' per nakshatra ≈ 1 day of Moon's travel
While the Western system tracks the Sun through the year, the Vedic system tracks the Moon through the lunar month with higher resolution.
Critical Insight:
27 = ONE ELEMENT-QUARTER of the 108-unit meta-cycle
27 × 4 = 108 (full meta-cycle of padas)
Each set of ~7 nakshatras corresponds to one element
The Moon's position in a nakshatra at birth (Janma Nakshatra) is considered more important than Sun sign in Vedic astrology because it describes the emotional/karmic nature — the Moon being the mind, the memory, the feeling self.
