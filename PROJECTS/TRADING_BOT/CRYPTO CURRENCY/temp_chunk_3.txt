PART VI: ZOROASTRIAN CYCLES — THE 12,000-YEAR DRAMA
The Structure of Time
Zoroastrianism presents history as a 12,000-year cosmic drama between Ahura Mazda (Wise Lord/Good) and Angra Mainyu (Destructive Spirit/Evil).
The Four 3,000-Year Periods
The Three Creations
Menog (Spiritual/Ideal Creation):
The first 3,000 years
Ahura Mazda creates the world in spiritual form
Everything exists in perfection, without mixture
Angra Mainyu is not yet active in creation
Getig (Material/Physical Creation):
Years 3,000-6,000
The spiritual forms take on material existence
Angra Mainyu attacks and mixes evil into creation
Death, disease, and decay enter the world
Present Period (Mixed State):
Years 6,000-12,000
The battleground of good and evil
Humanity plays a decisive role
Three Saoshyants appear to guide restoration
The Three Saoshyants (Saviors)
The final 3,000 years are divided into three millennia, each presided over by a Saoshyant born from Zarathushtra's miraculously preserved seed:
Frashokereti — The Final Renovation
The Process:
Battle: Final battle between forces of good and evil
Resurrection: All the dead are raised in their original bodies
Final Judgment: Ordeal by molten metal
The righteous experience it as warm milk
The wicked experience it as molten metal
Purification: All souls are purified
Renovation: World is made perfect forever
Eternity: Time ends; eternal perfection begins
From the Bundahishn:
"Ahura Mazda will seize the Evil Spirit, and Sraosha will seize Ahriman... then the Evil Spirit will be made powerless, and the creation of Ahura Mazda will be pure."
Correspondence with Other Traditions
The Uniqueness of Zoroastrian Eschatology
Unlike cyclic systems (Hindu, Mayan), Zoroastrianism is linear with a definitive end:
History has a beginning (creation) and an end (renovation)
Evil is finally and permanently defeated
Time itself ends; eternity begins
This influenced Jewish, Christian, and Islamic apocalypticism

PART VII: HOPI CYCLES — THE FOUR WORLDS
The Four (and Fifth) Worlds
The Hopi Cosmology
The Hopi believe the Earth has been created and destroyed multiple times. We currently live in the Fourth World, with the Fifth World approaching.
First World — Tokpela (Endless Space)
Element: Fire (or spiritual fire) Destruction: Fire/Volcanic activity/Comet strike
Characteristics:
First creation by Taiowa (Creator) through Sotuknang (nephew)
Humans lived in harmony with animals
Some humans forgot the Creator, became materialistic
The obedient ones were led underground by Ant People
The world was destroyed by fire (volcanoes, possibly a comet)
Second World — Tokpa (Dark Midnight)
Element: Ice Destruction: Earth's axis shifted; frozen
Characteristics:
Humans emerged from the underground
Again forgot the Creator over time
Became greedy and warlike
Saved ones again protected by Ant People
Earth's axis tilted; world frozen in ice
Third World — Kuskurza
Element: Water Destruction: Great flood
Characteristics:
Advanced civilization developed
Great cities built
People forgot spiritual ways again
