## **HOW TO READ THIS DOCUMENT**

This section covers the manuscript's COSMOLOGICAL pages — the famous NINE-ROSETTE FOLDOUT MAP that serves as the SYSTEM DIAGRAM for the entire Voynich pharmaceutical operation. It occupies folios 85–86, which form a large multi-panel foldout that, when fully opened, displays nine circular rosette structures connected by pathways.

**The Nine-Rosette Map:**

* 9 circular rosette structures \= 9 MAJOR PROCESS DOMAINS or facilities  
* Central rosette \= CORE OPERATION (the heart of the system)  
* Surrounding rosettes \= supporting procedures (inputs, outputs, storage, specialized processing)  
* Pathways/causeways between rosettes \= MATERIAL FLOW and PROCEDURAL DEPENDENCIES  
* Text blocks on each panel \= OPERATIONAL SPECIFICATIONS for that region

**How the sections map to rosettes (proposed):**

* **Herbal rosettes:** Plant sourcing, identification, harvesting (Earth element inputs)  
* **Astrological rosettes:** Timing and scheduling (celestial governance)  
* **Balneological rosettes:** Purification and circulation (Water element processing)  
* **Pharmaceutical rosettes:** Compounding, formulation, packaging (Fire element outputs)  
* **Central rosette:** Integration — where all inputs converge and the final product emerges

**Physical Structure:** The foldout consists of SIX PANELS (three recto, three verso surfaces visible when unfolded). Text blocks appear on multiple panels, each associated with specific rosette regions. Some panels contain primarily illustration with labels; others (like f85r1 and f86v6) contain dense operational text.

**Note on transcription gaps:** The rosette foldout is one of the most damaged and difficult-to-read portions of the manuscript. Several panels (f85r2, the main rosette face) have limited or no EVA transcription available in the standard interlinear archive. The text blocks that ARE transcribed provide the operational specifications for the system.
