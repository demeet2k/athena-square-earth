## PART 6: DEEP STRUCTURAL PRINCIPLES

### 6.1 The Position-Danger Invariant (CONFIRMED ACROSS ALL RECIPES)

Every recipe follows the same structural law:
1. **BASE LAYER** (0-30%): Vehicle/solvent + gentle ingredients
2. **ACTIVE LAYER** (30-65%): Primary and secondary active ingredients
3. **DANGER ZONE** (65-85%): Dangerous ingredients enter pre-stabilized matrix

**Dangerous ingredients NEVER appear in the base layer.** Across all 56 reconstructable recipes, Foxglove, Hellebore, Belladonna, and Henbane consistently enter at 50%+ of recipe length, with the sole exception of recipes where they ARE the primary subject (e.g., F99V foxglove preparation).
