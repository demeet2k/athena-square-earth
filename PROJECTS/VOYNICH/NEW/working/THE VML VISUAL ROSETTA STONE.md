## **II.4 THE UMBRELLA/PINEAPPLE AS ALCHEMICAL APPARATUS**

The "umbrella" form \= **ALEMBIC HEAD** (condensation cap). Vapors rise, hit dome, condense, drip down. The "pineapple" form \= **DISTRIBUTION MANIFOLD** (shower head). Single stream divided into multiple fine streams. When combined \= **COMPLETE REFLUX DISTILLATION COLUMN**: vapors rise into umbrella (condense) → pass through pineapple (distribute) → enter pool (react) → reheat, repeat. Functionally identical to fractional distillation column.

**VML mapping:** Umbrella \= she- (transition/infuse) — vapor becomes liquid. Pineapple \= ss-/qoqo- (intensify/circulate) — high-throughput distribution. Pool \= \-al/-ol (structure/fluid) — receiving reaction chamber. Rising vapor \= che- (volatile/spirit). Condensate drips \= \-dy (fixed) — vapor that has become liquid.

## **II.6 WHY TWO VISUAL GRAMMARS EXIST**

**Herbal \= SERIAL processing (one track):** Source → Process → Product. One input, one channel, one output.

**Bath \= PARALLEL NETWORK processing (multiple tracks):** Multiple inputs, multiple vessels, feedback loops, clamp gates, phase alternation, routing decisions, convergence.

The manuscript needed BOTH grammars: herbal for simple extraction sequences; bath for complex multi-vessel production operations.
