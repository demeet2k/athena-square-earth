# METRO LINES

## 1. Purpose

Metro lines are the high-frequency routes that repeatedly connect distant regions
of the corpus. They are the nervous system's reusable pathways.

## 4. Default Route Priority

When a task spans multiple regions:

1. start at `T3`,
2. choose one primary metro line,
3. identify one transfer hub,
4. branch into at most two secondary lines,
5. contract back through `T2` or `T1`.

This keeps the nervous system navigable instead of combinatorially noisy.
