Coordinate the deep swarm as a layered build that moves from elemental workers to archetypal clusters to 256-neuron crystal fields without collapsing the archive into flat summaries.

Tier 1:

## Convergence Rule

The swarm does not finish when many files exist. It finishes when:
1. nodes are addressable
2. routes are explicit
3. witness state is known
4. local outputs contract upward without contradiction

## Anti-Laziness Rule

Do not stop at folder creation. Each tier must add a real routing, synthesis, or verification advantage.
