# Synthesis 12 - Query, Oracle, and Decision

Depth position: `12/16`

Execution scale: `256^4` agents

Focus: formalize asking, measuring, deciding, and abstaining as mathematical acts.

## Sixteen new language seeds

### Square row

- `Query Object Algebra`: questions become typed objects.
- `Decision Ledger Logic`: decisions become auditable ledger events.
- `Measurement Prompt Calculus`: prompts become measurement operators.
- `Oracle Syntax Math`: oracle interfaces get a formal syntax.

### Flower row

- `Sampling Dynamics`: sampling changes a state and must be accounted.
- `Cast-and-Response Grammar`: a query and its response are one route.
- `Decision Flow Calculus`: choice becomes a transport process.
- `Remedy Route Dynamics`: answers produce legal remedy paths.

### Cloud row

- `Belief Corridor Math`: belief is corridor-scoped, not absolute.
- `Posterior Budget Algebra`: posterior updates spend evidence budget.
- `Ambiguity Selection Logic`: sometimes the correct choice is to preserve branches.
- `Abstention Decision Calculus`: refusal to collapse is lawful output.

### Fractal row

- `Inquiry Loop Arithmetic`: inquiry is recursive and cumulative.
- `Search Refinement Tower`: search deepens by structured passes.
- `Answer Replay Logic`: answers must be replayable under the same evidence.
- `Consultation Closure Algebra`: a consultation closes only when remedy and witness close.

## Hybrid generators
