### Flower row

- `Threshold Crossing Dynamics`: crossing a boundary is a first-class event.

- `Liminal Transition Calculus`: liminal motion becomes formal and totalized.
- `Repair Route Dynamics`: every blocked move emits a repair route or abstention.

### Cloud row
