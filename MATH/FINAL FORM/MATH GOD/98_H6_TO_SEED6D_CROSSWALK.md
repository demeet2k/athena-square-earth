- `shared_appendix_floor`

- `seed_6d`

- `reentry_routes`

These fields are interpreted as follows.

### lane_inputs

Names the current FIRE, Water, AIR, and Earth evidence surfaces that fed the shared center.

The minimum canonical appendix set required for the shared center before any route-specific
conditionals are added.

### h6_center

The compiled four-lane convergence state.
It binds the additive field but does not replace the base chapter or appendix grammar.

`1024^6` is a carrier descriptor only and never a replacement for chapter or appendix addresses.

### convergence_routes

The lane-to-lane routes that move from FIRE pressure into Water continuity, AIR registry,
Earth admissibility, and then into the shared center.

### reentry_routes

The lawful return routes from `Seed-6D` back into named canonical anchors.
