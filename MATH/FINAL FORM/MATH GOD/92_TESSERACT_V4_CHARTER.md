# MATH Tesseract v4 Charter

## Summary

This charter promotes the `FINAL FORM` math corpus into a MATH-specific tesseract routing layer without destroying the older atlas, pass, metro, or appendix work already present in `MATH GOD`.

The rule is non-destructive lift:

- keep the existing `4^4` local lattice law
- keep the older metro and pass surfaces as witness layers
- add deterministic chapter orbit, triangle rail, zero-point tunnel, and HCRL container routing on top
- keep planet, archetype, pillar, and mode systems as annotation overlays only

Google Docs remain blocked for this lift. The canonical evidence boundary is local only.

The Earth H6 lift is additive to this charter. It does not replace the v4 layer. It consumes the v4 outputs and adds Earth-owned contracts for legality, replay, pruning, dimension lift, and downstream handoff discipline.

The chapter station codes are fixed by padded base-4:

- `Ch01⟨0000⟩` through `Ch21⟨0110⟩`
