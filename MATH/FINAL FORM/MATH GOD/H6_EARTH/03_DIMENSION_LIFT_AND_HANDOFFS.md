- `3D`: containment chambers and support surfaces
- `4D`: the existing tesseract v4 route calculus
- `5D`: compressed Earth contract families `E1` through `E4`
- `6D`: the woven Earth hologram of chapter contracts, appendix contracts, handoff rules, and regeneration seed

This lift is additive. It does not rewrite the 4D base. It compresses and stabilizes it so other elemental lanes can receive disciplined outputs.

- Fire receives only Earth-cleared activation outputs

- Air receives only schema-stable proof-and-return outputs
