## Repeated Structural Matches

### Source to finite world

Every major tradition contains a move from undifferentiated source to articulated world.

### Operator beings

Every major tradition contains figures that act more like process-carriers than like simple characters.

### Query interfaces

Many traditions contain one or more authorized uncertainty-resolution mechanisms:

- divination
- omen reading
- astrology
- oracle procedure
- token selection

### Corrective practice

Most traditions contain a way to repair misalignment after diagnosis:

- sacrifice
- purification
- offering
- discipline
- prayer
- recalibration
- alchemical operation

### Final or stabilizing state

Most traditions contain an endpoint or asymptote of restored relation:

- liberation
- peace
- right order
- perfected stone
- return
- Omega

## Important Difference Between "Same" and "Isomorphic"

This folder does not claim that Vedic, Taoist, Greek, Ifa, Kabbalistic, Qur'anic, Voynich, and alchemical systems are identical.

It claims something weaker and more useful:

they can often be mapped onto a shared functional grammar with enough fidelity to support global comparison and dense compression.

That is why an isomorphism table is the right tool here.
