### 1.1 Etymology and Definitions

├── THEORETICAL KABBALAH (Kabbalah Iyyunit):
│   ├── Philosophical/contemplative
│   ├── Understanding the nature of God/creation

│   ├── OPERATIVE/MAGICAL
│   ├── Uses divine names for effects

    ├── Prophetic/experiential
    ├── Achieving altered states

    └── Overlaps with practical
