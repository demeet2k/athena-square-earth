### 3.1 Theory of Sigils

**What is a Sigil?**

```

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

A sigil is a graphical encoding of a magical intention
designed to bypass the conscious mind and implant
the desire directly into the subconscious.

KEY PRINCIPLES:

1. ENCODING
   ├── The conscious statement is transformed
   ├── Into abstract symbolic form
   ├── That cannot be read literally
   └── But contains the full intention

2. BYPASSING
   ├── The psychic censor cannot block
   ├── What it cannot recognize
   ├── The abstract form slips through
   └── While the meaning gets implanted

3. FORGETTING
   ├── After encoding and launching
   ├── The original intention must be forgotten
   ├── So consciousness doesn't interfere
   └── The subconscious does the work

4. LAUNCHING
   ├── The sigil is charged in gnosis
   ├── Then released into the unconscious
   ├── Via forgetting or destruction
   └── It works without further attention
```

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

         ───────────────────

├── G = Gnosis (intensity of altered state, 0-1)
├── L = Link (connection to target, 0-1)
├── A = Awareness (conscious interference, 0-1)

GNOSIS MATTERS:
├── Higher G = More effect
├── Deep gnosis essential

├── Knowing target increases L

├── Higher A = Less effect
├── Lust of result destroys magic
├── Must forget after launching
├── Psychic censor = high A
└── This is why sigils work

RESISTANCE:
├── Higher R = Less effect
├── Target can resist consciously
├── Shields increase R
├── Consent lowers R
└── Unknown target can't resist consciously

PROBABILITY:
├── Very unlikely events (low P) hard
├── Very likely events (high P) easy
├── Magic best near 50% probability
├── Don't try to levitate
└── Try for plausible outcomes
