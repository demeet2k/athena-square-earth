### 1.3 Sect (Hairesis)

```
THE DOCTRINE OF SECT:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

WHAT IT IS:
├── Division of planets into day/night teams
├── Fundamental organizing principle
├── Determines which planets support native
├── Affects interpretation significantly
├── Lost in modern astrology
└── Essential to Hellenistic practice

DAY SECT (Diurnal):
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Chart: Sun above horizon (day birth)
Leader: ☉ SUN
Benefic: ♃ JUPITER (Greater Benefic of sect)
Malefic: ♄ SATURN (Lesser Malefic - "in sect")

In day charts:
├── Jupiter most helpful
├── Saturn less harmful
├── Mars more problematic (out of sect)
└── Venus still good but less so

NIGHT SECT (Nocturnal):
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Chart: Sun below horizon (night birth)
Leader: ☽ MOON
Benefic: ♀ VENUS (Greater Benefic of sect)
Malefic: ♂ MARS (Lesser Malefic - "in sect")

In night charts:
├── Venus most helpful
├── Mars less harmful
├── Saturn more problematic (out of sect)
└── Jupiter still good but less so

SECT CONDITIONS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

A planet is "in sect" when:
1. Belonging to the sect of the chart (day/night)
2. In correct hemisphere:
   ├── Day planets: above horizon by day
   ├── Night planets: below horizon by night
3. In correct sign polarity:
   ├── Day planets: masculine signs
   └── Night planets: feminine signs

Maximum sect = all three conditions met
Planet "rejoices" when fully in sect
```
