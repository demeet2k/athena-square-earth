```
├── Hebrew, Arabic, Greek, Latin sources
├── Combined into systematic manuals

├── Greek original
├── Solomon's conversations with demons
├── Names 36 demons (decans)
├── Christian elements
├── More literary than practical
└── Sets the pattern

THE KEY OF SOLOMON (Clavicula Salomonis):
├── Multiple versions (13th century onward)
├── Most influential grimoire
├── Practical magical manual
├── Planetary magic
├── Pentacles and seals
├── Tools and consecrations
├── Spirit conjuration
└── The "Greater Key"

THE LESSER KEY OF SOLOMON (Lemegeton):
├── 17th century compilation
├── Five books:
│   ├── Goetia (72 demons)
│   ├── Theurgia-Goetia (aerial spirits)
│   ├── Ars Paulina (planetary)
│   ├── Ars Almadel (angelic)
│   └── Ars Notoria (knowledge)
└── Most famous for Goetia

ADDITIONAL SOLOMONIC TEXTS:
├── Sworn Book of Honorius (Liber Juratus)
├── Grimorium Verum
├── Grand Grimoire
├── Sixth and Seventh Books of Moses
├── Black Pullet (Poule Noire)
└── Various manuscripts
```
