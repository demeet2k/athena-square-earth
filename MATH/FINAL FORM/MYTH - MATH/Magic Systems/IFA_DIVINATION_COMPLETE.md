## Chapter 2: Divination Method

### 2.1 The Opele (Divining Chain)

```
THE OPELE (Ọ̀pẹ̀lẹ̀):
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

WHAT IT IS:
├── Chain of 8 seed halves
├── Linked together
├── Each half has concave/convex sides
├── Cast to reveal Odù
├── Faster than palm nuts
├── Most common method today
└── "Backbone of Orunmila"

STRUCTURE:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

○ ─ ○ ─ ○ ─ ○ │ ○ ─ ○ ─ ○ ─ ○
(right side)   │  (left side)
   4 seeds     │    4 seeds

├── Right side = senior Odù
├── Left side = junior Odù
├── Seeds fall convex (ǀǀ) or concave (ǀ)
├── Read from right to left within each half
└── Creates complete Odù

CASTING PROCEDURE:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. PREPARATION:
   ├── Clean casting surface (Ọpọ́n Ifá)
   ├── Mark sacred powder (iyerosun)
   ├── Prayer to Orunmila
   ├── Feed Eshu first
   └── Client states question/issue

2. CAST:
   ├── Hold opele by center
   ├── Prayer/invocation
   ├── Drop onto surface
   ├── Chains fall naturally
   └── Read the pattern

3. READING:
   ├── Note concave (ǀ) or convex (ǀǀ)
   ├── Right side gives senior Odù
   ├── Left side gives junior Odù
   ├── Identify the full Odù
   └── Begin interpretation

EXAMPLE:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

If cast shows:
Right: concave, convex, convex, convex
Left: convex, convex, convex, convex

Right = ǀ ǀǀ ǀǀ ǀǀ = Irosun
Left = ǀǀ ǀǀ ǀǀ ǀǀ = Oyeku

Odù = Irosun-Oyeku
```
