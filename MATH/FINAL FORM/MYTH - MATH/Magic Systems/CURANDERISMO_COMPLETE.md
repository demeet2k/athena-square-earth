### 2.1 La Limpia (Spiritual Cleansing)

```
LIMPIAS (Cleansings):
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

WHAT IT IS:
├── Spiritual cleansing
├── Remove negative energy
├── Remove illness
├── Remove curses/hexes
├── Purification
├── Most common treatment
└── Many methods

LIMPIA CON HUEVO (Egg Cleansing):
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PROCEDURE:
1. Fresh raw egg (room temperature)
2. Pray over patient and egg
3. Rub egg over entire body
   ├── Start at head
   ├── Down to feet
   ├── Don't miss joints, armpits
   └── May cross repeatedly
4. Egg absorbs negativity
5. Break egg into glass of water
6. Read the egg
7. Dispose properly (toilet, crossroads)

READING THE EGG:
├── Bubbles: Evil eye (mal de ojo)
├── Blood spots: Strong envy
├── Cloudy white: Negative energy
├── Cooked appearance: Very strong mal
├── Webs/threads: Gossip, entanglement
├── Eye shape: Someone looking/watching
├── Points/spikes: Enemies
├── Clear: Patient is clean
└── Multiple readings show progress

LIMPIA CON HIERBAS (Herb Cleansing):
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
├── Bundle of fresh herbs
├── Common: Ruda, albahaca, romero, pirúl
├── Sweep body from head to toe
├── Prayers while sweeping
├── May be wet or dry
├── Herbs absorb negativity
├── Dispose by burning or burying
└── Often combined with other methods

OTHER LIMPIAS:
├── Con sahumerio: Smoke/incense cleansing
├── Con fuego: Fire cleansing (candle flames)
├── Con agua bendita: Holy water
├── Con limón: Lemon
├── Con ajo: Garlic
├── Con chile: Chili pepper
└── Con piedras: Stones
```
