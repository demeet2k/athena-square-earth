## Chapter 1: What the PGM Is

### 1.1 Historical Context

```
THE GREEK MAGICAL PAPYRI:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

WHAT IT IS:
├── Collection of magical texts
├── Found in Egypt
├── Dating: 2nd century BCE - 5th century CE
├── Written in: Greek, Demotic Egyptian, Coptic
├── Contains: Spells, rituals, hymns, recipes
├── Represents: Greco-Egyptian syncretism
└── Primary source for practical ancient magic

THE CORPUS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Main Collections:
├── PGM I-LXXXI (Greek texts)
├── PDM (Demotic magical papyri)
├── Supplementary texts discovered later
├── ~600+ distinct spells and procedures
└── Largest ancient magical collection

DISCOVERY:
├── Most found in Thebes, Egypt
├── 19th century discoveries
├── Giovanni Anastasi collection (1828)
├── Now in various museums:
│   ├── Leiden (Netherlands)
│   ├── London (British Museum)
│   ├── Paris (Bibliothèque Nationale)
│   └── Berlin (Egyptian Museum)
└── Published: Preisendanz (1928-1931)

CULTURAL SYNTHESIS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

The PGM represents fusion of:
├── EGYPTIAN: Isis, Osiris, Thoth, Horus
├── GREEK: Zeus, Hermes, Apollo, Helios
├── JEWISH: YHVH, IAO, Adonai, angels
├── BABYLONIAN: Stellar magic, demons
├── PERSIAN: Mithras, cosmic powers
└── GNOSTIC: Aeons, archons, secret names

This syncretism defines late antiquity magic.
```

### 1.3 Voces Magicae (Words of Power)

```
VOCES MAGICAE (Magical Words):
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

├── Strings of seemingly nonsensical syllables
├── Often transliterated from other languages
├── Hebrew divine names
├── Egyptian deity names
├── Created through various formulae
├── Have inherent power when spoken
└── Not meant to be "understood"

TYPES OF VOCES MAGICAE:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

DIVINE NAMES (Recognizable):
├── IAO, IAEO, IAOUE (variants of YHVH)
├── ADONAI, ELOAI, SABAOTH (Hebrew titles)
├── ABRASAX, ABLANATHANALBA
├── AKRAMACHAMAREI
├── SESENGENBARPHARANGES
└── Transmitted with variations

PALINDROMES:
├── Read same forwards and backwards
├── ABLANATHANALBA
├── ABERAMENTHŌOULERTHEXANAXETHRELTHYOOMENTHÊRABAR
├── Represent cosmic completeness
└── Create closed magical circuits

VOWEL STRINGS:
├── Seven Greek vowels = seven planets
├── A E Ē I O Y Ō (α ε η ι ο υ ω)
├── Various combinations and permutations
├── AEEIOUO, IEOAUO, etc.
├── Sung or intoned during ritual
└── Create harmonic resonance

CHARACTERISTIC STRINGS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Common elements found throughout PGM:

"ABERAMENTHŌOULERTHEXANAXETHRELTHYOOMENTHÊRABAR"
├── Long palindrome
├── Appears in many texts
├── Associated with solar/cosmic power
└── Ultimate power name

"AKRAMACHAMAREI"
├── Name of great power
├── Often combined with others
└── Possibly Egyptian origin

"SESENGENBARPHARANGES"
├── Name found frequently
├── Nature debated
├── Powerful invocation name
└── Used in major operations

THE SEVEN VOWELS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

A (Alpha)   = Moon
E (Epsilon) = Mercury
Ē (Eta)     = Venus
I (Iota)    = Sun
O (Omicron) = Mars
Y (Upsilon) = Jupiter
Ō (Omega)   = Saturn

COMBINATIONS:
├── All seven in sequence = cosmic invocation
├── Specific arrangements for specific planets
├── Sung in rising or falling tones
├── Create "music of the spheres"
└── Central to many rituals
```
