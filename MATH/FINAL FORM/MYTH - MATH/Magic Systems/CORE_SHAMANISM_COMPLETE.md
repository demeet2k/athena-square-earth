### 2.2 Journey Technique

```
THE SHAMANIC JOURNEY:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PREPARATION:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. SETTING:
   ├── Quiet, darkened space
   ├── Comfortable position (lying best)
   ├── Eye cover (bandana)
   ├── Drum or recording ready
   └── Uninterrupted time (15-30 min)

2. INTENTION:
   ├── Clear purpose for journey
   ├── Specific question or task
   ├── "I am journeying to [world] to [purpose]"
   ├── Stated clearly before start
   └── Focused throughout

3. STARTING PLACE:
   ├── Visualization of entry point
   ├── For Lower World: hole, cave, tree roots
   ├── For Upper World: tree top, mountain, beam of light
   ├── Memorize your personal entry
   └── Use same one consistently

THE JOURNEY:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. INDUCTION:
   ├── Begin drumming (4-7 beats/second)
   ├── Or start recording
   ├── Close eyes
   ├── Visualize starting place
   └── State intention silently

2. ENTRY (Lower World):
   ├── See your entry (hole, cave, etc.)
   ├── Enter and descend
   ├── May feel like tunnel
   ├── Eventually emerge into landscape
   └── You are in Lower World

3. EXPLORATION:
   ├── Look around
   ├── Notice environment
   ├── Call for power animal/guide
   ├── Wait for spirit to appear
   ├── Ask it to help with intention
   ├── Follow its guidance
   └── Trust what you receive

4. RECEIVING:
   ├── Information may come as:
   │   ├── Words/thoughts
   │   ├── Images/symbols
   │   ├── Feelings
   │   ├── Direct knowing
   │   └── Experiences
   ├── Don't analyze during journey
   ├── Just receive
   └── Remember everything

5. RETURN:
   ├── Callback signal (faster drumming)
   ├── Thank spirits
   ├── Return the way you came
   ├── Ascend through tunnel/entry
   ├── Return to body
   ├── Wait for drumming to stop
   ├── Lie still, breathe
   └── Open eyes slowly

POST-JOURNEY:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
├── Record immediately (journal)
├── All details, no matter how small
├── Do not interpret yet
├── Ground yourself (eat, walk, etc.)
├── Reflect on meaning later
├── Note recurring symbols
└── Follow up on guidance received
```
