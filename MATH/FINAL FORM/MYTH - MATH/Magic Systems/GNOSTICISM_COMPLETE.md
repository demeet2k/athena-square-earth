## Chapter 1: What Gnosticism Is

├── Diverse ancient religious movements
├── Salvation through knowledge (gnosis)
├── Greek "gnosis" = knowledge/knowing
├── Not intellectual knowledge
├── Direct experiential knowing
├── Revealed, mystical knowledge
├── Self-knowledge = divine knowledge
└── "Know yourself" → Know God

├── Creator god (Demiurge) ≠ true God
├── True God = distant, unknown
├── Knowledge liberates the spark
├── Dualistic worldview
├── Complex mythological systems
├── Salvation through awakening
└── Secret/esoteric teachings

MAJOR SCHOOLS:
