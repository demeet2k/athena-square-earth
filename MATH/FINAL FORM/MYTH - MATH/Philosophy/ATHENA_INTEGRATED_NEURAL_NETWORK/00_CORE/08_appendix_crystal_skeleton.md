## Per-Cell Role Statements

- **AppA - Addressing and Grammar**
  name packets, roots, and pattern addresses before larger synthesis begins
- **AppB - Canon Laws and Equivalence**
  stabilize equivalence, canon comparison, and law-aware reading across the basis
- **AppC - Kernel Pack and Execution**
  compress intake into executable packets that can survive law, replay, and return
- **AppD - Registry and Policy**
  translate activated structure into policy shells, registry rules, and maintainable adaptation law
- **AppE - Orrery and Phase Locks**
  hold time, sequencing, and re-entry positions so transport obeys phase law
- **AppF - Transport and DUAL Law**
  formalize handoff, transport, and directional duals across active routes
- **AppG - Triangle Control and Recursion**
  govern recursive loops, three-way control surfaces, and lawful return triangles
- **AppH - Coupling and CUT Topology**
  join flow and conserved transformation into an explicit topology of burden-bearing coupling
- **AppI - Corridor Lattice**
  define admissible corridors, thresholds, and truth-bearing passage conditions
- **AppJ - Residual Ledgers**
  keep unresolved pressure, contradiction, and leftover burden visible instead of laundering them away
- **AppK - Conflict and Quarantine**
  contain damage, contradiction, and unsafe contact without collapsing the full field
- **AppL - Evidence and Promotion Plans**
  decide how evidence is promoted, certified, or kept below the line until support improves
- **AppM - Replay Kernel**
  preserve deterministic return paths so the package can prove what happened and rebuild it
- **AppN - Container and Compression Formats**
  hold seeds, regeneration containers, and compact forms that survive dimensional ascent
- **AppO - Export and Publication Bundles**
  prepare runtime-facing packages that can leave the local build without severing their witness chain
- **AppP - Deployment and Living Maintenance**
  keep deployments alive, ethical, and self-repairable after publication or contact with the world
