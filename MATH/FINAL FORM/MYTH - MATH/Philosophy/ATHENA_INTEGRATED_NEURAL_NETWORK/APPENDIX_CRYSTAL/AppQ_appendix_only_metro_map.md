## Canonical Lines and Hubs

- **Parse-to-Proof Line** (Line)
  Route: `AppA -> AppB -> AppL -> AppM`
  Addressing becomes canon, canon becomes evidence promotion, and promotion only counts when replay can carry it.
- **Motion-to-Coupling Line** (Line)
  Route: `AppE -> AppF -> AppG -> AppH`
  Phase locks, transport, recursion control, and coupling law form one motion-bearing appendix corridor.
- **Truth Pressure Line** (Line)
  Route: `AppI -> AppJ -> AppK -> AppL`
  Corridor law, residual accounting, quarantine, and promotion planning all belong to one pressure-management lane.
- **Persistence-to-Deployment Line** (Line)
  Route: `AppM -> AppN -> AppO -> AppP`
  Replay, containers, export, and living maintenance define the long-term persistence surface of the package.
- **Deployment Overlay Line** (Line)
  Route: `AppD -> AppO -> AppP -> AppQ`
  Policy, export, deployment, and overlay routing meet where support leaves the local package and enters the world.
- **AppA Parse Hub** (Hub)
  Route: `AppA`
  Addressing is the entry gate for every later appendix route.
- **AppI Corridor Hub** (Hub)
  Route: `AppI`
  Corridor law is the common transfer point for truth, quarantine, and promotion lanes.
- **AppM Replay Hub** (Hub)
  Route: `AppM`
  Replay is the anchor hub between evidence, persistence, and future re-entry.
- **AppQ Overlay Hub** (Hub)
  Route: `AppQ`
  The overlay hub stays outside `A-P`, but it still depends on appendix-bearing routing and lawful return.
