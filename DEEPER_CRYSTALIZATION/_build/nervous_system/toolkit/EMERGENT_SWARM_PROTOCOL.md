## Mission

Coordinate the deep swarm as a layered build that moves from elemental workers to archetypal clusters to 256-neuron crystal fields without collapsing the archive into flat summaries.

## Tiers

- one kernel coordinator

Tier 1:
- 4 elemental workers: Earth, Water, Fire, Air

Tier 2:
- 16 archetype cells: 4 pillars plus 12 mixed off-diagonal agents

Tier 3:
- 64 cluster nodes: each archetype split again into four local subfunctions

Tier 4:

## Higher Overlays

- Silver overlay: 8-way refinement when four elements are too coarse
- Bronze overlay: 12-agent pantheon for richer inter-agent role tagging
- Copper overlay: 16-field control surface aligned to the appendix grid

## Convergence Rule

The swarm does not finish when many files exist. It finishes when:
1. nodes are addressable
2. routes are explicit
3. witness state is known
4. local outputs contract upward without contradiction

## Anti-Laziness Rule

Do not stop at folder creation. Each tier must add a real routing, synthesis, or verification advantage.
