## Wheel Rotation Mechanics

Each wheel rotates through its layer content in a deterministic order. The rotation is not a metaphor — it is the execution order of the compiler when it traverses the organism.

- **W1 rotation**: Intro emits seed -> E10 receives return. The rotation is instantaneous: emission and reception are the same act viewed from two ends of the circuit.
- **W3 rotation**: Su -> Me -> Sa -> Su. Three turns, each of seven chapters. The rotation is forward by default; reverse reading (Sa -> Me -> Su) is legal and reveals the governance view.
- **W5 rotation**: A (hub) -> B-F (first spoke) -> G-K (second spoke) -> L-P (third spoke) -> A. The hub is always present; the three spokes rotate.
- **W7 rotation**: K-Q (first canopy arc) -> R-X (second canopy arc) -> Y-Z (hinge pair) -> K. The hinge pair connects the canopy to the crown.
- **W9 rotation**: E1-E3 (first crown triad) -> E4-E6 (second crown triad) -> E7-E9 (third crown triad) -> E1. The 3x3 matrix reads as three triads or as three columns; both orderings are legal.
