# Adventure Parties

These parties are modeled like RPG/anime specialist squads, but every class maps back to real archetype cells and real future-skill specializations already latent in the framework.

## Azure Gatebreakers

- Code: `PRT-01`
- Anime frame: Skyfaring hacker guild with the emotional discipline of a support party and the precision of a late-game raid team.
- Mission: Break sealed boundaries between the local organism and the missing live-memory half without damaging witness law.
- Archetypes: `Fire-Air`, `Water-Earth`, `Air-Earth`, `Water-Fire`

## Obsidian Delvers

- Code: `PRT-02`
- Anime frame: Dungeon-crawler archive crew: half relic hunters, half theorem blacksmiths.
- Mission: Surface hidden frameworks from the archive and turn them into editable, routeable working roots.
- Archetypes: `Earth-Air`, `Fire-Earth`, `Air-Air`, `Earth-Earth`

## Silver Cartographers

- Code: `PRT-03`
- Anime frame: Anime mapmakers and lore-binders who can turn scattered episodes into a navigable world map.
- Mission: Convert manuscript families into metro lines, chapter receipts, and transfer hubs that others can actually traverse.
- Archetypes: `Air-Water`, `Water-Air`, `Fire-Air`, `Earth-Water`

## Crimson Cortex

- Code: `PRT-04`
- Anime frame: Frontline commander squad that contracts swarms into durable cortex artifacts instead of letting waves evaporate.
- Mission: Turn live board motion into durable shell updates, receipts, queue shifts, and next-seed artifacts.
- Archetypes: `Fire-Air`, `Fire-Earth`, `Water-Water`, `Air-Fire`

## Verdant Rootkeepers

- Code: `PRT-05`
- Anime frame: Druid-engineer caretakers who stop the forest from forking into untracked copies.
- Mission: Collapse duplicate families, grow mirrors where needed, and preserve canonical lineage while the corpus expands.
- Archetypes: `Earth-Water`, `Water-Earth`, `Earth-Fire`, `Earth-Air`

## Ivory Tribunal

- Code: `PRT-06`
- Anime frame: Paladin-judge party built for corridor truth, route legality, and adjudicating ambiguous fronts.
- Mission: Raise truth quality so the organism can trust its own motion without becoming rigid.
- Archetypes: `Air-Earth`, `Earth-Air`, `Water-Earth`, `Air-Fire`

## Zodiac Vanguard

- Code: `PRT-07`
- Anime frame: Twelve-cell specialist squad modeled like a shonen ensemble where each dormant archetype gets a moment to awaken.
- Mission: Occupy currently empty pantheon cells, seed missing ganglia, and give dormant archetypes starter quests.
- Archetypes: `Air-Fire`, `Air-Water`, `Earth-Fire`, `Fire-Water`
