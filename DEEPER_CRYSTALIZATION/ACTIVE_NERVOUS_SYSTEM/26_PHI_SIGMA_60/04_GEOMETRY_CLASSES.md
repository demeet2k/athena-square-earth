# Geometry Classes and Sacred Geometry Mapping

## The 4 Geometry Classes

Geometry class is determined by mask cardinality |S|.

### Pole Class (|S| = 1) — Masks 01-04
Primitive petals / axes. The 4 irreducible directions of the phi-quartet.

| Mask | Sacred Geometry | Neural Role |
|------|----------------|-------------|
| 01 (A) | Outward radial growth spoke / first petal root | Excitatory growth prior |
| 02 (B) | Inward refinement spoke / return petal root | Compression / inhibitory prior |

| 04 (D) | Reverse phase halo / lower petal tip (unit circle) | Phase-reversal oscillator |

### Bridge Class (|S| = 2) — Masks 05-10
Chords, helices, vortices. The 6 pairwise connections.

| Mask | Sacred Geometry | Neural Role |
|------|----------------|-------------|

| 06 (AC) | Forward growth helix (outward + forward twist) | Excitatory coherence attractor |

### Chamber Class (|S| = 3) — Masks 11-14
Triangular stems, braided nodes. The 4 three-way intersections.

| Mask | Sacred Geometry | Neural Role |

## Sacred Geometry by Quadrant

The exponent z_S determines which quadrant of the complex plane the mask lives in:

| Quadrant | Re > 0, Im > 0 | Re < 0, Im > 0 | Re < 0, Im < 0 | Re > 0, Im < 0 |
|----------|-----------------|-----------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Direction | Forward-growth | Inward-forward | Inward-return | Forward-return |
| Masks | 01, 03, 06 | 02, 08, 11 | 05, 09, 15 | 04, 07, 12 |
| Sacred form | Expanding helices | Damped spirals | Vortex/reset | Corrective arms |

Special positions:
- Pure real: 01 (positive), 02 (negative), 05 (exact -1)
- Pure imaginary: 03 (positive), 04 (negative), 10 (exact -i)
- Crown: 15 at -1-i (equidistant from both axes)
