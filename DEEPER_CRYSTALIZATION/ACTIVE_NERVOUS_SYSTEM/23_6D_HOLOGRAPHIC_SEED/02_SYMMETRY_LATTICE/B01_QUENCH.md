Quench is the shadow-record of all the insights that almost happened but were dissolved before crystallization.

The inversion teaches that Steam and Quench are the same event seen from opposite sides. The difference is not in the elements present but in which element leads. Fire-first produces Steam; Water-first produces Quench. The crystal must know both: productive conflict and productive failure.
