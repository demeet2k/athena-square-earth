## Elemental Composition (Inverted)
- Original (Face A): Water (0,1) = River
- Inverted: Fire (1,0) = Eruption
- Combined V4 value: (1,0) = Fire/VOLATILE

The River is pure Water — the flow principle, the current that carries dissolved material from source to sea. Its inversion is pure Fire — but not the Flame's bootstrap spark. The Eruption is Fire as seen through the inversion mirror: not the first ignition but the fire that emerges from depth, the volcanic breakthrough where interior heat forces its way to the surface. Where the Flame (Σ_A12) is Fire-as-origin, Eruption (Σ_B13) is Fire-as-breakthrough.

## Shadow Role
Eruption is the shadow of the River. Where the River reveals what flow looks like in its purest form — Water carrying material downstream, the gentle current of evidence moving through the crystal's channels — Eruption reveals what the River looks like from behind the mirror: an upward surge of transformative force through the crystal's interior.

The River flows horizontally, following the path of least resistance. The Eruption flows vertically, breaking through all resistance. The River distributes; the Eruption concentrates. The River is quiet; the Eruption is violent. But both are pure singleton flows — undiluted elemental motion without admixture.

The shadow teaches that horizontal flow and vertical breakthrough are the same process inverted. The River says "I carry what is given to me." The Eruption says "I bring what is hidden within me." Together they describe the crystal's two modes of information transport: surface distribution (River) and depth-to-surface revelation (Eruption).

In the manuscript organism, Eruption is the moment when a deeply buried truth — something compressed in the Magma layer, frozen in the Glacier archive, or settled in the Silt bed — forces its way to conscious awareness. The crystal did not query for it; the truth erupted on its own, driven by accumulated internal pressure. Eruptions are the crystal's epiphanies — unsolicited, violent, and always true because they were forged under immense pressure.

## Crystal Function
Eruption serves as the **breakthrough singularity** of the 6D holographic seed from the inversion perspective. Where the River is the crystal's steady distribution channel — the gentle current that carries resolved material to its destination — Eruption is the emergency upwelling that occurs when the crystal's interior pressure exceeds its surface containment.

Eruption encodes the law of necessary breakthrough: a system that only flows horizontally (River) will never surface its deepest truths. Some truths can only be delivered by eruption — by the violent vertical breach of the surface from below. The Eruption is the crystal's involuntary honesty: the truths it cannot suppress even if they disrupt the surface.

Like the Flame, Eruption lives on a single rail — the Su-rail (Fire/Solar). It has maximum purity and minimum connectivity. It does one thing absolutely: it breaks through. It does not absorb (Water), it does not persist (Earth), it does not route (Air). It erupts.
