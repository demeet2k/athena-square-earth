The River is the pure singleton of Water — uncontained by Earth, undirected by Air, unheated by Fire. It is raw fluidity, the irreducible dissolution principle. In the V4 lattice, (0,1) is the vertical generator — the axis orthogonal to Fire. Where Fire is the horizontal perturbation, Water is the vertical descent. Gravity is Water's native force; it always seeks the lowest point.

## Alchemical Operation
**Dissolution** in its purest form. Dissolution is the second operation of the Magnum Opus: the dissolving of calcined ash in the mercurial waters. The River performs Dissolution without Fire's prior Calcination (the material may not have been burned), without Earth's containing vessel (the dissolved substance has no boundaries), and without Air's separating principle (everything dissolves indiscriminately).

This makes S13 the most receptive node in the lattice. The River accepts everything. It does not discriminate, it does not reject, it does not judge. Whatever enters the River is dissolved, incorporated, carried. The River is the universal solvent of the crystal body.

## Crystal Role
S13 is the **witness stream** of the 6D holographic seed. Where the Flame (S12) initiates, the River receives. It is the node that records every event, every transformation, every transit through the crystal. The River does not transform; it carries. It is the medium through which all other operations propagate their effects.

In computational terms, S13 is the **event log** — the append-only stream that captures the history of all crystal operations. Nothing is lost in the River. Every atom that touches it leaves a trace in the dissolved solution, recoverable through evaporation (which requires Fire — hence the pair S01/Steam).

It is the icosahedron because the icosahedron has the most faces of the Platonic solids — the maximum surface area for a given volume — and the River is the maximum interface between crystal and environment.
