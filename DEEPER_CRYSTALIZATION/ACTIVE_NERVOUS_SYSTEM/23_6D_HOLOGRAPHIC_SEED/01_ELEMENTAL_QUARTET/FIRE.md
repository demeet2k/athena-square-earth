Every token stream that crosses the boundary of the tome does so because Fire deemed it lawful. The ParseKernel is the axiomatic ignition: deterministic, bounded, absolute. Nothing enters without burning through its predicate.

From the gate, Fire leaps to theory. The **DocumentEntity** (Ch06) transforms raw admitted content into theory-bearing shells — documents that are not merely containers but living hypotheses. Here Fire acts as the alchemist's crucible: what enters as evidence emerges as theorem. Every document becomes a theory, every theory becomes a testable flame that either illuminates or consumes itself upon verification.

The flame then synchronizes. **OrbitCalculus** (Ch08) governs the timing of Fire's pulse — the operator S that binds latent-core semantics z across documents, ensuring that when multiple flames burn simultaneously, their rhythms cohere. Fire then sharpens into architecture. The **CUTModule** (Ch15) — the Computation Universe Toolkit — is the blade that Fire forges from its own heat. Here transformation becomes executable: API contracts, system interfaces, implementable specifications. The CUTModule is the point where Fire stops being metaphor and becomes machinery, where the illuminating principle crystallizes into code that runs.

Deployed into the world, Fire accepts its boundaries. **AgencyBound** (Ch17) governs the corridors within which Fire may act externally — cloud limbs, execution workers, governed action.
