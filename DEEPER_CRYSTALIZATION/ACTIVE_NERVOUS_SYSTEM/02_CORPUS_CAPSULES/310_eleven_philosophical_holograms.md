# Capsule 310 — 11 Philosophical Holograms Overview

**Source**: 2026-03-18_holographic_rosetta_stone.md
**Family**: holographic_rosetta
**Lens**: F (Flower/Dynamics)

Eleven philosophical traditions are identified as complete operator-holograms, each compressing the whole crystal through a different dominant archetypal axis. Pre-Socratics discover primitive source-operators (arche, logos, being, opposition, cycle, atom, void, nous). Plato stabilizes the pattern-field through Forms, the Good, and consciousness ascent. Aristotle stabilizes embodied causation through categories, substance/accident, form/matter, four causes, and potentiality/actuality.

Stoicism stabilizes lawful agency: impression, assent, control, alignment, reserve, acceptance. Epicureanism stabilizes local composition and relief through discrete units, clinamen, desire-filtering, and tranquility. Neoplatonism stabilizes vertical return: One -> Nous -> Soul -> world, then purification, contemplation, theurgy, henosis.

Phenomenology stabilizes givenness: intentionality, bracketing, constitution, horizon, lifeworld. Existentialism stabilizes self-authorship: existence before essence, radical freedom, bad faith, authenticity. Pragmatism stabilizes testing and correction: consequences, inquiry, community consensus. Structuralism/Post-Structuralism stabilizes and destabilizes the symbolic code itself. Process Philosophy stabilizes becoming: occasions, prehension, concrescence, creativity, transmission.

## Key Objects
- 11 philosophical holograms with distinct operator-languages
- Invariant archetypal roles: source, pattern, process, law, relation, agency, valuation, return
- Each school foregrounds a different aspect of the same crystal

## Key Laws
- Traditions disagree about which role is most fundamental; the disagreement gives the higher-D map
- Each tradition contributes genuinely non-redundant information
- The crystal is the same; the dominant read-head changes
