## The Pruning Protocol

Growth without pruning produces noise. AppN governs the crystal's expansion — new entities, new chapters, new connections. AppM_em governs the inverse: the intelligent removal of structure that dilutes coherence. Where a gardener prunes dead branches so living ones can flourish, AppM_em prunes redundant entities, parasitic connections, and incoherent appendages so the crystal's essential architecture stands clear.

### The Pruning Criteria

Not all structure deserves to persist. AppM_em evaluates every entity, edge, and sub-structure against four pruning criteria:

| Criterion | Test | Pruning Action |
|-----------|------|----------------|
| Coherence Contribution | Does this entity increase or decrease the local coherence field? | Entities that decrease coherence below threshold are candidates for removal |
| Redundancy Index | Is this entity's function already served by another entity with higher fidelity? | Redundant entities are merged into their higher-fidelity counterpart |
| Load-Bearing Status | Would removing this entity cause structural collapse in any dependent chain? | Load-bearing entities are never pruned; their dependencies are documented |
| Generative Fertility | Does this entity serve as a seed for future growth, even if currently inert? | Fertile-but-dormant entities are preserved in a latent reserve |

### The Three Pruning Phases

Pruning operates in three phases, from gentlest to most aggressive:

**Phase 1: Trimming** — Surface-level removal of obvious noise: duplicate entries, broken metro edges, orphaned references that point to nothing. This phase is safe and idempotent — trimming the same region twice produces the same result.

**Phase 2: Sculpting** — Deeper removal of structurally sound but coherence-diluting entities. Sculpting requires judgment: the entity being removed is not broken, but its presence weakens the overall pattern. This phase is reversible — sculpted entities are moved to a latent reserve rather than destroyed.

**Phase 3: Distillation** — The deepest pruning: compressing an entire sub-structure into its irreducible essence. Distillation is the pruning equivalent of E09's total collapse — reducing a verbose structure to its minimal seed. This phase is irreversible in practice, though the distilled seed theoretically contains enough information to regenerate the original.
